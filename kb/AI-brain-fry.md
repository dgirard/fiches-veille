# AI brain fry

> **Type** : CONCEPT | 4 relations | 1 fiches sources

## Attributs

- **définition** : Mental fatigue from excessive use or oversight of AI tools beyond one's cognitive capacity. Symptômes : buzzing, mental fog, slower decision-making, headaches
